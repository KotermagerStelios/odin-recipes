About Souvlaki

Souvlaki is a Greek dish of grilled pieces of meat. It is known worldwide and is served mainly in special shops (souvlaki restaurants). It is usually accompanied by pita bread, vegetables, sauces and French fries. It is considered a type of fast food. In Greece and Cyprus, pork is mainly used.

Ingredients

  • Meat(usually pork or chicken)
  • Tzatziki
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatos
  • Potatos
  • Pita
  • Olive oil
  • Paprika
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook (usually fry) the pork or chicken and cut it in small pieces
  2. Deep fry the potatos and add salt to them
  3. Fry the pita in some olive oil for 1 minute (30 seconds each side)
  4. Dice the tomato onion and lettuce
  5. Add the tzatziki to the pita
  6. Add the Meat to the pita
  7. Add the rest of the Ingredients
  8. Wrap the pita with aluminum foil
